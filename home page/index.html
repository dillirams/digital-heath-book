OTHER SECTOR OF HEALTH BOOK

TELEMEDICINE

Telemedicine utilizes technology, such as video conferencing and remote monitoring, to provide virtual healthcare consultations and services. It enables patients to receive medical advice, diagnosis, and treatment from the comfort of their homes.

GENOME AND
PRECISION MEDICINE

Genomics and precision medicine involve analyzing an individual's genetic information to customize medical care and treatment plans based on their genetic makeup.

MHEALTH

mHealth, or mobile health, refers to mobile devices, such as smartphones and tablets, to deliver healthcare services, access health information, and promote healthy behaviors.

HEALTH ANALYTICS
AND BIGDATA

Health analytics and big data involve collecting, analyzing, and interpreting large volumes of health-related data to derive insights and support evidence-based decision-making in healthcare.

WEARABLE MEDICAL
DEVICES

Wearable medical devices are sensor-based devices that individuals can wear to monitor various health parameters and collect real-time data about their well-being.

HOW DIGITAL HEALTH
BOOK WORKS

  • The doctor scans the patient's QR code which enables the doctor to access, update and save the patient's health record directly in cloud.

  • Patients can access their health records through the Digital Health Book app but are restricted from making changes to the records.

  • The AI analyzes the user's health records and prompts the patient for feedback on their well-being, provides recommendation on steps to improve health and AI sends reminders to patients for medication timings

  • The Digital Health Book includes a media section that covers the latest health issues, news, and campaigns organized by health authorities or organizations

  • Patients can engage with healthcare professionals via the Digital Health Book app, seeking medical guidance, discussing treatment options, and receiving online consultations, thereby improving accessibility to healthcare services.
